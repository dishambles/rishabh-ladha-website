Now · 2024 – Present

EPIC World — Head of GTM

Elevar Equity platform India

I joined EPIC World as Head of GTM to work on what I think is the most interesting B2B opportunity in India right now — building a generation of companies that actually serve the ~$100 trillion economic engine of entrepreneurial households.

EPIC's thesis: India's growth over the next 20 years will be powered by the households that run the chai shop, the kirana, the logistics business, the local clinic. We're benchmarking the public companies solving for them through the EHI Index — 34 companies, combined market cap $115Bn — and helping build the next 50 private companies that should be on it.

Then · 2015 – 2024

SquadStack — Co-founder & Chief Business Officer

Enterprise telesales India + US ₹190Cr+ raised Forbes 30 Under 30

I co-founded SquadStack (originally SquadRun) in 2015 with three people and no plan to be in sales. We started as a future-of-work platform — combining AI and human intelligence for content moderation and internet safety work — and evolved into the leading sales talent marketplace in India.

We raised ₹190Cr+ across rounds from Bertelsmann India, Chiratae Ventures, and Blume Ventures. We were named Forbes 30 Under 30 along the way. I spent nine years there, most of them on the revenue side, building enterprise GTM motions for clients across BFSI, MSME lending, real estate, and healthcare.
